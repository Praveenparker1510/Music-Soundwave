About SoundWave

A free music platform built for everyone — listeners, creators, and dreamers.

SoundWave was built with one simple mission: music should be free and accessible to everyone. No paywalls, no subscriptions, no ads.

We curate thousands of Creative Commons songs from incredible independent artists around the world — from jazz pianists to electronic producers to folk storytellers.

Every track on SoundWave is legally free to stream, download, and use in your own projects under Creative Commons licensing.

Licensing & Usage

All music on SoundWave is released under Creative Commons licenses. This means you can freely stream and download for personal use. For commercial use, please check the specific license for each track on the original source platform.

Artists featured include Kai Engel, Kevin MacLeod, Josh Woodward, Ketsa, Broke For Free, Rolemusic, and Blue Dot Sessions — all incredible musicians who chose to share their work with the world.
